About the Project

A Reading Collective Built Different

Booklings CSN is not a boring book club — it's a community of sharp, curious students who gather weekly to read critically, debate interpretations, and publish insightful notes. We built them a complete digital platform with gamified participation, curated reading lists, session archives, and a digital bookshelf spanning philosophy, fiction, and critical essays.

Key Features

What We Built

Every feature was designed to make literary engagement rewarding, social, and structured.

XP, Tiers & Streaks

Every session gives XP. Students level up, maintain streaks, unlock roles, and earn visible recognition in the community.

Razorpay Payments

Subscription-based membership with full Razorpay integration for seamless payment processing and plan management.

Digital Bookshelf

Interactive bookshelf with hover previews featuring titles from 1984 to Meditations — foundational texts curated each semester.

Thought Experiments

Curated paradoxes and debate topics — Ship of Theseus, Trolley Problem, Simulation Hypothesis — sparking deep weekly discussions.

Session Gallery

Visual archive of meetings and events, giving members a visual journey through their reading collective history.

Member Applications

Curated onboarding — students apply, moderators review, and accepted members join an engaged, high-quality reading circle.
